About Me

Hello, I’m Jaya Shree!

Hello and welcome! I’m a passionate mobile photographer who finds joy in capturing the beauty of the world around me — whether it's the intricate patterns of nature, the expressions of animals, or the quiet, fleeting moments of everyday life. My journey began with something simple — a photo of a single leaf drenched in raindrops, taken on a rainy day with just my mobile phone. That single click opened up a new way of seeing the world. I began to realize that photography isn’t about the gear — it’s about the perspective, the emotion, and the story that every image can tell. As I continued exploring, my mobile phone became my constant companion, always ready to frame the wonders I stumbled upon — a sunset spilling gold across the sky, a puppy mid-play, or a butterfly gently resting on a flower. Each click is more than just an image — it’s a memory, a feeling, a piece of life frozen in time. JS Clickz is my creative space — a personal gallery where I share what I see, feel, and love. Through my lens, I hope to reveal the magic in the ordinary and the emotions in the unnoticed. Thank you for visiting.
